Aligning with Sustainable Development Goal 4

Inclusivity and Equity: Sage's design to be free and user-determined supports the goal of inclusivity by ensuring that anyone, regardless of economic status or location, can access quality educational materials.

Lifelong Learning: By catering to users from grade school to adulthood, Sage embodies the spirit of lifelong learning, encouraging users to continue their education at any stage of life.

Personalization: With functionality to create tailored learning environments, Sage addresses the diverse needs and learning styles of individuals, which is essential for effective education.

Global Reach: The focus on leveraging internet access aligns with the need to bridge the digital divide and extend learning opportunities to underserved populations worldwide.

Skill Development: By covering a range of skills from basic literacy to advanced technical skills, Sage helps users become more employable and better equipped to participate in the modern economy.

Target 4.1: Complete primary and secondary education

Indicator 4.1.1: Proportion of children achieving minimum proficiency levels in reading and mathematics.

Indicator 4.1.2: Completion rate in primary and secondary education.

How Sage addresses Target 4.1: Sage curates educational content to support students in achieving proficiency in core subjects, aiming to improve completion rates in primary and secondary education.

Target 4.2: Access to quality early childhood development

For young learners, Sage can offer curated educational content that aligns with developmental milestones, thereby fostering readiness for primary education.

Target 4.3: Equal access to technical, vocational, and tertiary education

Sage's platform can be used to guide learners of all ages to technical, vocational, and tertiary educational resources, thereby promoting equal access for all users, regardless of background.

Target 4.4: Increase the number of youth and adults with relevant skills

By offering resources on a variety of skills, from basic to technical and vocational, Sage supports users in acquiring the skills needed for employment and entrepreneurship.

Target 4.5: Eliminate gender disparities in education

Sage, by being universally accessible, helps bridge the gender gap in education by providing the same quality resources to both males and females, as well as to other vulnerable groups.

Target 4.6: Achieve literacy and numeracy

The platform can provide literacy and numeracy resources, supporting users to achieve at least a minimum proficiency level in these fundamental areas.

Target 4.7: Acquire knowledge and skills for sustainable development

Sage can incorporate sustainable development topics into its content library, thus equipping users with the knowledge and skills to promote sustainable development, human rights, and gender equality.

Target 4.a: Build and upgrade education facilities

Although Sage is a virtual platform, it can contribute to this target by providing a safe, inclusive, and effective online learning environment that complements physical educational facilities.

Target 4.b: Expand scholarships for developing countries

By connecting users with educational opportunities and potentially scholarship information, Sage can play a role in expanding educational opportunities for users in developing countries.

Target 4.c: Increase the supply of qualified teachers

Sage can serve as a platform for connecting learners with qualified teachers and mentors, thus increasing the availability of quality educators, especially if it incorporates a feature for international cooperation and teacher training.
